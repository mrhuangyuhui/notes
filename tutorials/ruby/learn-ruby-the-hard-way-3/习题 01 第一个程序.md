# 习题 1 第一个程序

```ruby
puts "Hello World!"
puts "Hello Again"
puts "I like typing this."
puts "This is fun."
puts 'Yay! printing.'
puts "I'd much rather you 'not'."
puts 'I "said" do not touch this.'
```

输出结果：
```
Hello World!
Hello Again
I like typing this.
This is fun.
Yay! printing.
I'd much rather you 'not'.
I "said" do not touch this.
```

**注意：如果遇到 ASCII 编码错误，在脚本的最上面加入：`# -*- coding:utf-8 -*-`。**
